INTRODUCTION

Asa Palmilk (M) Sdn Bhd ditubuhkan pada 5hb Julai 2007. Syarikat ini di miliki sepenuhnya oleh Bumiputera Melayu dengan berobjektifkan perniagaan berasaskan pembuatan,penghilangan serta pengedaran makanan buaab tempatan khasnya produk-produk cokelat.

Asa Palmilk (M) Sdb Bhd was incorporated on 5th July 2007 and is fully 100% own by Malay Bumiputera individuals. its core bussiness is processing, manufacturing and distributing locally made chocolate products.

Syarikat ini diterjui oleh golongan professional dan usahawan bumiputera yang berpengalaman luas dalam mentadbir dan mengendalikan syarikat serta penghilangan. Syarikat ini juga mempunyai kaki tangan teknikal yang berpengalaman dan pernah terlibat di dalam kerja pembuatan, penghilangan serta pengedaran produk-produk makanan khasnya makanan halal jenama antarangsa.

the company is managedand administered by professionals and successful enterpreneur with proven track records and exensive experiece in business and manufacturing industry. equipped under employmen is strong technical staffing with experiences in processing, manufacturing and distribution of international branded food produtcs.

Berasaskan keupayaan kwangan yang stabil serta sokong dari pada pembekal-pembekal serta usaha gigih, Asa Palmilk (M) Sdn. Bhd akan terus bertapak sebagai sebuah syarikat pembuatan makanan halal khasnya sapuan Cokelat yang kukuh dan terus berkmbang sejajar dengan industri pembuatan makanan halal di negara ini.

With sufficient financial capabilities and strong support form suppliers, it's believe that with greater effort Asa Palmilk (M) Sdn Bhd could be well poise as the next fundamental player in chocolate industries in tandem with the strong growth in halal foot manufacturing scenario in Malaysia.

COCO-V BACKGROUND

Asa Palmilk (M) Sdn Bhd began its journey of operations in 2010 focusing on processing, manufacturing and distributing locally made Coco-V Hazelnut and Chocolate Spread. The production is run in our own factory located in Pulau Indah, while the operation team, administered by professionals with proven track records and extensive experience in business and manufacturing industri, is based in Wisma Dato' Shaari in Port Klang.

Since the initial production, the company has expanded its market share and aggressive distribution efforts of Coco-V to a number of malaysia's leading hypermarkets competing with various big names the market.

Coco-v that comes in two delicious flavours, Hazelnut and Chocolate are produced using well selected premium qualty intredients esuring consumers' satisfaction towards our irresistible indulgence. Coco-V is formulated to become part of the combination of a balanced breakfast that whole families will enjoy, especially as it contains no artificial colouring or preservatives.

Being the pioneer among the Muslims to produce chocolate spread with JAKIM's halal certification and utmost support by definitive goals, Coco-V could well be poised as the next fundamental product in chocolate industries in tandem with the strong growth in halal food manufacturing industries in Malaysia and globally.
